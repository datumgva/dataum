Our mission:

The future of the small enterprise is in data of the organisation and of their competitors. Today, to gain a competitive advantage we will need to harness this data. If we are not doing it, our competitors are. It is survival of the Bittest. At Datum, we aim to guide small organisations that do not have access to the required technical expertise through the process of analysing, selecting and implementing the most appropriate systems to enable them to win the data war.

Our Methodology:

Once our partnership has been established, we will work together through the entire process. Our aim is to make the journey smooth with constant feedback loops to ensure a final product that will give your organisation a significant competitive advantage.

Our offerings:

Data analysis

Machine learning, artificial intelligence to find patterns in the data. Viable products will then be built on these patterns.

Paperless office

Scan and catalogue all your paper documents allowing more users that need access to these documents to work from home.

Automation

Analyse your current manual workflows and find repeatable patterns. A system is then programmed allowing the automation of these manual processes.

Our technology

Our expertise is spread over numerous different technologies, but we always use best of breed open source applications which are ideally suited to the small enterpise.
